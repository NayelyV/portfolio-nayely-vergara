About me

My name is Nayely Vergara. I’m a developer. I help my customers to find innovative solutions to their problems. I have worked with companies and brands all over the world, ranging from little startups to national telecoms, governmental institutions and banks.

Education

Graduated from the Faculty of Engineering of the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM) in 2012 as a Systems Engineer. Since then, I have taken various courses, such as "Scrum Master" and "Product Owner" at Agile Academy, "Project Management" at Netec and constant training in English, programming languages and new technologies. In 2020 and until the end of 2021 I studied the Online Master in App Design: UX / UI at the Barcelona School of Design (ESDESIGN).

Experience

I have 9 years of experience in programming and development of mobile applications with languages such as Swift and Kotlin, as well as various frameworks from Facebook, Google, Crashlytics, among others. Among the applications I have led are MediQó, Grin, Host a Pet, Nutrigram, The Cage. In September 2018, together with my co-associate, we decided to open Osyres, our own product development and design agency. Since then, my role as CEO allows me to create close relationships with clients, conceptualize projects and develop native mobile applications on Android and iOs, such as Aprissa, Gluup, Invex, EAP 362.
